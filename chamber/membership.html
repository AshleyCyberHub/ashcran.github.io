Membership Levels

Non-profit - $100

This membership level includes your business added in to the chamber of commerce directory

Bronze - $200

This membership includes your business added in to the chamber of commerce directory and two tickets to the annual dinner.

Silver - $400

This membership includes your business added in to the chamber of commerce directory, four tickets to the annual dinner, and business training for one.

Gold - $1000

This membership includes your business added into the chamber of commerce directory, four tickets to the annual dinner, business training for two, membership plaque, advertising at town events, and advertisement on the chamber of commerce website.
